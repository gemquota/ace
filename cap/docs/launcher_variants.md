# 🚀 CAP.OS // V3.1.0 LAUNCHER VARIANTS (SWARM_GRID EDITION)

These five variants represent the "Boot/Launcher" UI for CAP OS. This is the first screen seen when the system initializes the Mesh.

---

## 📟 1. THE BRUTAL_TERMINAL (Retro-Functionalist)
**Vibe:** Fallout 'RobCo' Terminal meets Bloomberg Professional.
- **Visuals:** High-contrast Amber or Green text on absolute black. No images, only ASCII-art logos.
- **Data Density:** Extreme. A scrolling 4-column "Mesh Heartbeat" table shows every active node, its Tailscale IP, and current CPU temp in real-time.
- **Interaction:** Command-line prompt at the bottom of the dashboard. You "type" to launch subsystems (e.g., `boot --clide`).
- **Emoji:** 📟 🛰️ 🧪 🛡️

---

## 🛰️ 2. THE GRID_COMMANDER (Tactical Map)
**Vibe:** Global War Room / Satellite Tracking.
- **Visuals:** A world map (or stylized mesh map) in the background. Nodes (Termux, Windows, etc.) are glowing dots connected by pulsing "Tailscale Tunnels".
- **Data Density:** Geographical. Hovering over a node shows its `operator_manifest.json` flags. A bottom-dock shows the Celery DAG queue as a horizontal scrolling "film strip".
- **Interaction:** Click a node to "Remote Into" its terminal. Toggle switches for Redis and Celery status.
- **Emoji:** 🌍 📡 ⚡ 🔗

---

## 🧬 3. THE KERNEL_BIOS (Low-Level Diagnostic)
**Vibe:** A futuristic BIOS/UEFI setup screen.
- **Visuals:** Blue and White text boxes with thin borders. Very structured and rigid.
- **Data Density:** System-heavy. Shows raw HEX values of the Genesis Hash, the exact versioning strings from `semver`, and a "Memory Pressure" gauge for the Redis broker.
- **Interaction:** Tab-based navigation. You "Provision" nodes by checking boxes and hitting a large "INITIALIZE_SINGULARITY" button.
- **Emoji:** 🧬 💾 🗝️ ⚙️

---

## 🎰 4. THE MARKET_TICKER (Economic Focus)
**Vibe:** Robinhood / Crypto Exchange.
- **Visuals:** Dark mode with neon highlights. A massive, scrolling horizontal "Intent Ticker" at the top showing the price of recent tasks.
- **Data Density:** Financial. Large "Balance" cards for each agent. A "Profit/Loss" graph showing system utility over the last 24 hours.
- **Interaction:** "Buy" and "Sell" buttons for compute cycles. Launching the OS feels like "Opening the Market".
- **Emoji:** 💎 📈 🪙 🎰

---

## 🌌 5. THE NEBULA_DASH (Glassmorphic Immersive)
**Vibe:** Modern macOS / iOS "Control Center" in deep space.
- **Visuals:** Heavy glassmorphism (frosted glass), blurred space background, sleek rounded corners.
- **Data Density:** "Floating" widgets. Vitals pulse in translucent bubbles. The `changelog.md` appears as a beautifully formatted "Story" feed on the right.
- **Interaction:** Gestural. Drag and drop "Capability Chips" onto nodes to assign tasks.
- **Emoji:** 🌌 🫧 🧊 🚀
