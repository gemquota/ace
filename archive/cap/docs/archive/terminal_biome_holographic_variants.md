# 🌌 CAP.OS // THE BIOME-HOLOGRAPHIC SYNTHESIS

These variants merge the **Organic/Borderless Biome (5.3)** with the **Depth-Centric Holographic (5.5)** paradigm. All designs feature zero box-drawing characters, relying on ANSI gradients, whitespace, and temporal fading to create structure.

---

## 🌫️ 1. THE NEURAL_VOID
**Concept:** Data emerging from and dissolving into an infinite field.
- **Visuals:** The terminal edges are vertical heatmaps of shifting Braille patterns (`⡷⣧⢾`) in dark gray. 
- **Depth:** New events in the **Waterfall** and active nodes in the **Constellation** "pop" in bright, bold white. Over 10 seconds, they fade through vibrant green, then dim forest green, then deep charcoal, eventually vanishing.
- **Vibe:** The machine feels like a living nebula. 🌌

---

## ⚡ 2. THE SYNAPTIC_SHIMMER
**Concept:** Emphasizing the "Threads" of causality.
- **Visuals:** When a task executes, a shimmering, multi-colored ASCII "Synapse" (`~·-·>`) connects the agent node to its log entry. 
- **Dynamics:** The synapse pulses at the frequency of the **Kernel Pulse HUD**. 
- **Holographic Effect:** The background layer (Constellation) sits at a permanent 50% opacity, while the foreground (Waterfall) uses 100% brightness. Causality lines bridge the depth gap. 🔗

---

## 🌊 3. THE CHROMATIC_HORIZON
**Concept:** Using color temperature to indicate "Heat" and "Age".
- **Visuals:** Active areas of the screen use a "Hot" palette (Bright Cyan/White). Older data clusters shift toward "Cool" colors (Deep Blue/Purple) before fading to black.
- **Structure:** No columns. The **Constellation** hardware map sits in the center. The **Waterfall** logs wrap around it in a circular or "spiral" fashion, utilizing depth-shading to prevent overlap. 🌀

---

## 🕵️ 4. THE LAYERED_OPERATOR
**Concept:** Interactive depth focusing using `[TAB]`.
- **Visuals:** Two distinct focus planes.
- **Mode A (Node Focus):** The **Constellation** is bright and sharp; the **Waterfall** logs are blurred/dimmed into the background.
- **Mode B (Log Focus):** The **Waterfall** text is crisp and intense; the nodes drift into a hazy, out-of-focus background shimmer.
- **Interaction:** Tapping `[TAB]` triggers a high-speed ANSI cross-fade between the layers. 🎹

---

## 🧿 5. THE SINGULARITY_PULSE (Final Synthesis)
**Concept:** The ultimate merger of all features.
- **Visuals:** Vertical **Bio-Rhythm** margins acting as system load indicators.
- **Graph:** A borderless **Constellation** where agents "orbit" the central **Kernel Pulse**.
- **Execution:** When a goal is injected, the **Waterfall** "rains" down from the top, with each line connected to its parent node via a fading holographic thread.
- **Integrity:** Causal breaks or anomalies cause the specific node and its entire branch to vibrate (rapid ANSI color cycling), drawing immediate operator focus. 🧪
